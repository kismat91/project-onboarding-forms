How to Join Our Classroom

Follow these simple steps to join our Google Classroom and start learning:

  • Go to Google Classroom and sign in with your Google account.
  • Click on the '+' button on the top right side of the screen and select 'Join class'.
  • Enter the class code "3ce5jmn". This code is a unique identifier for your class. Do not share this code with anyone outside of your class.
  • Click 'Join'. You are now added to the class!
